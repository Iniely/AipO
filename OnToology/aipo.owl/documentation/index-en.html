Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

Ontology that describes the Pensum of the Administration and International Business career of the Universidad Peruana Unión

Introduction back to ToC

Aipo is an academic ontology whose purpose is to conceptualize terms such as: Study plan (pensum), academic entities, graduation profile from the administration and international business career of an Adventist university. The ontology is intended to be transferable and reusable for the institutions within the denomination.

Main Classes back to ToC

The main Aipo clases are: Person, Course and Syllabus.

Person class back to ToC

The Student and Professor classes belong to Person class. Figure 2 shows an example of the Professor Class. Professor class has the folowing object properties: hasDegree, instructorOf, hasNationality and worksFor. The object property hasAcademicStaff belongs to "School Class" and hasInstructor corresponds to Course Class.
 
Figure 2: Professor Class Student class has the following object properties: hasNationality, hasCompeted, enrolledIn, attendsCourse and alumnusof
 
Figura 3: Student Class

Course Class back to ToC

Course Class is the heart of the ontology, all classes are linked to it directly or indirectly. Figure 4, shows an example of Course Class. The course S7-FEP is partOf Semester7 and is partOf gradutation profile "Profile_ANI". S7-FEP develops competencies: M-C3 (belongs To Marketing Knowledge Area), E-C3 and E-C4 (belong to Emprendedorismo Knowledge Area). G3-C1 (belongs to Gestion Empresarial Knowledge Area) an F-C2(belongs to Fiananzas Knowledge Area) are competencies that are partiallyDevelops by the course. S7-Fep minorDevelops competencies: I-C1, I-C2, I-C3 (belong to Research KnowledgeArea), F-C3 (belongs to Finanzas Knowledge Area), M-C2 (belongs to Marketing Nowledge Area) and E-C1 (belongs to Emprendedorismo Knowledge Area) Furthermore S7-FEP course, isLinked to Strategic Axis: Research (Inv15) and Integral Formation (Pe-8). Finally, S7-FEP course hasPrerequisite S6-FinzI and hasSyllabus S7-FEP_Syl.
 
Figura 4: Course class

Syllabus Class back To ToC

Syllabus class contains epecific information about Course class. Syllabus Class is linked to 7 classes: Course, TeachingMethod, Resource, EvaluationMethod, Topic and LearningOutcome. Figure 5, shows and example of Syllabus Class. Syllabus "S7-FEP_syl" is (syllabusOf) S7-FEP course, hasSupervisor Maritza Arana and Julio Rengifo. The topics T-FEPI1, TFEPI2, TFPI3, TFEPI4 y TFEPI5 are taughtIn S7-FEP_Syl. LO-8 is the learnig outcome acquiredinCourse S7-FEP_Syl
